# Seed

Store seed data, seed manifests, or seed-loading helpers here when they become
repo-owned graph operational assets.

## Current concrete asset

- `demo_fixture_seed_inventory.md`: documents the current accepted posture in
	which seed-like graph inputs still live under `demo/fixtures/` and are owned
	by the maintained demo/operator workflow rather than by a separate Neo4j seed
	loader.

At the current checkpoint, this folder is primarily documenting seed ownership
and promotion rules rather than storing a standalone seed bundle.
